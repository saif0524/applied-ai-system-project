[General Care Guide]
Dogs generally benefit from daily exercise, mental enrichment, and consistent feeding schedules.

[Medication Safety]
Medication tasks should be treated as required and tied to feeding time when instructions require food.

[Cat Care Basics]
Cats need litter maintenance, hydration checks, and short play sessions spread across the day.

[Senior Pet Notes]
Senior pets may need shorter, more frequent walks and closer monitoring for fatigue.

[Routine Reliability]
A routine is more sustainable when high-priority and required tasks are done first within available time.
